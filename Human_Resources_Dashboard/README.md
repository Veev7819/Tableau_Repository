##  Overview
TThis Tableau dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of employee trends between **2015 and 2024**, focusing on workforce size, attrition, demographics, location distribution, and salary insights.  
The goal is to support **HR and leadership decision-making** through data-driven workforce analysis.

The project is suitable for executive decision-making, stakeholder handover, and portfolio demonstration.

## Key Workforce Metrics
- **Total Employees Hired:** 8,950  
- **Active Employees:** 7,984  
- **Terminated Employees:** 966  
- **Highest Annual Terminations:**  
- **2017**, with **205 terminations**, indicating a significant attrition spike

## Demographic Analysis

### Education & Age (Active Employees)
- **60% (4,809 employees)** hold a **Bachelor’s Degree**
- Predominantly within the **35–44 age group**
- Job performance for this group is generally rated as **Good**

##  Location Insights
- **New York Office:** 6,270 active employees  
- Represents **79% of the total active workforce**
This concentration suggests operational efficiency but also introduces **geographical risk**.

## Strategic HR Recommendations
- Diversify Location Strategy
With 79% of employees based in New York, expanding into other regions can reduce operational risk and broaden talent acquisition.
- Investigate 2017 Attrition Spike
Conduct root-cause analysis on the 2017 termination surge to prevent future workforce instability.
- Plan for Retirement Transition
High-earning finance managers aged 50+ may approach retirement soon.
Implement succession planning and mentorship programs to preserve institutional knowledge.
