##  Overview
This Tableau dashboard analyzes the global freelance marketplace using data from **1,950 freelancers** across multiple platforms.  
The analysis explores **platform performance, earnings potential, rehire behavior, client satisfaction, and marketing impact**, providing insights for freelancers, platforms, and agencies.

## Dataset Summary
- **Total Freelancers:** 1,950  
- **Experience Distribution:**
  - Beginner: 34%
  - Intermediate: 33%
  - Expert: 33%

This balanced distribution allows for fair comparisons across experience levels.

##  Key Insights

### Platform Performance
- All major platforms achieved **job success rates above 70%**
- **Freelancer.com** recorded the highest success rate at **75.69%**
- Other platforms analyzed include:
  - Upwork
  - Fiverr
  - PeoplePerHour
  - Toptal

### Earnings & Pay Rates
- **App Development** emerged as the **top-earning category**
  - Average earnings: **$5,201.45**
- **Content Writing** had the **highest hourly rates**
  - Exceeded **$80/hour**

This highlights the importance of **specialization and niche selection**.

### Rehire Patterns
- Average rehire rates ranged between **40% and 50%**
- Rehire consistency observed across:
  - Job categories
  - Experience levels

This suggests rehire behavior is influenced more by performance and visibility than seniority alone.

### Client Ratings
- Average client ratings were consistently around **4.0 / 5**
- **USA-based freelancers** recorded slightly higher ratings at **4.06**

### Marketing Spend & Rehire Correlation
- A positive correlation was observed between **marketing spend and rehire likelihood**
- Freelancers who invested more in marketing experienced higher repeat engagement

## Key Recommendations
**For Freelancers** 
- Invest in marketing and visibility to improve rehire rates
- Focus on high-value niches such as:
App Development
Content Writing
Prioritize platforms with higher success rates

**For Platforms & Agencies**
- Provide upskilling opportunities for beginner freelancers
- Promote high-paying job categories to attract top talent
- Improve marketplace quality through targeted job placement strategies
